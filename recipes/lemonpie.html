Lemon Pie

Let's make Lemon Pie!

This is a brazillian-variant of the lemon pie recipe. The ingrients are more on the simpler side, but the tasty is top-notch!

Recipe taken from Tudo Gostoso.

Ingrients

Crust

  • 200g of cornstach biscuit
  • 150g of salted butter

Filling

  • 395g of condensed milk
  • 200g of milk cream
  • 4 lemons juiced
  • Lemon zest

Topping

  • 3 or 4 egg whites
  • 3 tbsp of sugar
  • Lemon zest

Steps

  1. Crust:

    1. Heat the oven at 180°C
    2. Grind the cornstach biscuit in a blender or processor into powder.
    3. Add the butter and mix it.
    4. Pour the dough into a pie pan with removable bottom and spread it.
    5. Bake it for approximately 10 minutes.

  2. Filling:

    1. Blend all filling ingreints in a blender until it is smooth and firm.
    2. Fill the cooked dough and take it to the fridge for 30 minutes.

  3. Topping:

    1. Beat the egg whites and sugar till it form a firm merengue.
    2. Pour it on top of the pie and arrange it.
    3. Put the lemon zest in it.
    4. Remove the pie from the pan and it is done!
